HMerge:About: Unity & Struggle began as Black NewArk, the local newspaper of the Committee For Unified Newark (CFUN), in April 1968. When the Congress of Afrikan People was established in early 1974 the paper's name was changed to Unity & Struggle. The paper continued under that name when the organization changed its name to the Revolutionary Communist League (M-L-M).
